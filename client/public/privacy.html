Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Flow Forcasting is a Progressive Web App (PWA). We do not collect, store, sell, or rent personal data. The app performs Monte Carlo simulations locally in your browser.

Data we collect

We do not collect personal information, identifiers, precise location, or usage analytics. Data you enter (throughput, cycle times, scenarios) stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

How the app works

  • Offline-first: after the first load, the app can work offline via a Service Worker cache.
  • No sign-in, no user accounts, no advertisements, no tracking SDKs.

Hosting & server logs

The web app is hosted on Vercel. Like most hosting providers, Vercel may process standard web server logs (e.g., IP address, user agent, error logs) to operate and secure the service. We do not link these logs to an identifiable person and we do not combine them with other data.

Third-party content

The app may load static assets such as web fonts or icons from reputable CDNs. These assets do not identify you personally.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children.

Your choices

You may clear the app’s cached data at any time via your browser settings (site data / storage) if you wish to remove locally stored information.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
